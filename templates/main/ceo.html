Khaotungkulmethee Pattawee: Co-Founder and Chairman

Full Name: Khaotungkulmethee Pattawee (Thai: ค่าตังกุลเมธี พัฒวี)

Other Name: Drake

Date of Birth: March 5, 2004

Place of Birth: Chiang Mai, Thailand

Nationality: Thai/Ugandan

Occupations:

  • Software Developer
  • Author and Writer

Years Active: 2021 - Present

Meet Khaotungkulmethee Pattawee Drake, one of the visionary entrepreneurs, and the driving force behind Redo Developers Inc. As a Co-Founder, and Chairman, he leads the company with a passion for innovation and a commitment to excellence.

Under Pattawee's leadership, Redo Developers Inc. has evolved from its inception in 2021 as Redo IO into a dynamic and influential player in the technology and software industry. His visionary approach has led to the company's rebranding in June 2023, expanding its services to pioneer immersive social experiences and cutting-edge technology solutions.

Pattawee's journey in technology commenced in the enchanting city of Chiang Mai, Thailand. With a strong foundation in computer science, he embarked on a global quest for knowledge and innovation. In 2021, he relocated to Makerere, Uganda, to further his studies in Medical Laboratory Technology, a testament to his dedication to both technology and healthcare.

As a tech visionary, Pattawee envisions a world where technology seamlessly integrates into daily life, creating meaningful and efficient solutions. His leadership has transformed challenges into opportunities, resulting in groundbreaking solutions that enhance user experiences, streamline workflows, and push the boundaries of innovation.

Experience the dynamic fusion of technology, innovation, and immersive social experiences crafted by Pattawee and the Redo Developers Inc. team. Join us in exploring the future of innovation and discover how Pattawee's leadership is reshaping the world of technology and software solutions.
