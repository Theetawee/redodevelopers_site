Install Progressive Web App (PWA)

Progressive Web Apps (PWAs) offer a convenient way to access our platform on your device. Here's how to install it on different devices:

Install PWA on iPhone:

  1. Open Safari on your iPhone.
  2. Visit Space website.
  3. Tap the "Share" button at the bottom center of the screen.
  4. Scroll down and select "Add to Home Screen."
  5. Customize the app name if desired and tap "Add."

Install PWA on Other Devices:

  1. Open your preferred browser on your device.
  2. Visit Space website.
  3. Look for the browser's menu or settings.
  4. Choose the "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App" option.
  5. Follow the prompts to add it to your home screen.
